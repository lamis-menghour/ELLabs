What is ELLabs?

ELLabs is an acronym for Embedded Learning Laboratories and it is a research and consulting office that aims to develop and enhance the fields of Computer Science (CS) and Information Technologies (IT) , by creating educational curricula and professional training courses, as well as focusing on the development of integrating technologies And provide the latter in educational institutions and companies operating in the sector.
